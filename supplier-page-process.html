Process Guideline

  1.  Order Issuance

Starting the process by issuing the order

  1. Raw Material Reception and Preparation

Description: Receiving raw materials, cleaning them, and preparing them for production.

  1.  Initial Heating

Heating the materials to a specific temperature to facilitate shaping or coating.

  1. Initial Loading

 Transferring materials to the production line.

  1. Main Distribution Area

Gathering and distributing materials.

  1. Loading to Coating and Surface Treatment Area

Gathering and distributing materials.

  1. Powder Coating

Applying protective or decorative coatings.

  1. Oven

Curing the coating for durability.

  1. Packaging

Wrapping and securing the finished product.

  1. Final Quality Check Before Shipping

Thorough inspection of product quality.

  1. Final Quality Check Before Shipping

Preparing shipping documents and logistics.

  1. Final Loading

Loading products for transportation.

  1. Delivery

Transporting the product to the customer.
